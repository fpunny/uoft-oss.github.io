Society

UofT Open Source Society is a student organization which aims to provide students with the opportunity to gain open source project experience for their resume and portfolio, while also encouraging them to work as part of a student led software development team.

Membership

  • You can join anytime at one of our events.
  • Membership in Open Source Society is open to all students, alumni, staff and faculty of the University of Toronto
  • Top contributors will be featured in the organization website as a valued member of the organization for the rest of the active year.
